# Chuck TV Series References Explained

All OrionAI modules are named after elements from the TV series "Chuck" (2007-2012). This document explains each reference for those unfamiliar with the show.

## Main Characters

### Chuck Bartowski
The protagonist - a regular electronics store employee who accidentally downloads the Intersect (a classified CIA/NSA supercomputer) into his brain, giving him instant access to government secrets and special skills.

### Sarah Walker (CIA Agent)
Chuck's handler and love interest. Frequently tells Chuck to "Stay in the car" when things get dangerous.

### John Casey (NSA Agent)
Strict, by-the-book NSA agent who follows rigid security protocols. Known for his no-nonsense approach.

### Morgan Grimes
Chuck's best friend - talkative, overshares, provides comic relief through verbose commentary.
